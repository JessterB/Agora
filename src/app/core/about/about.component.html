How to Access the Data

1

Have an account on Synapse. When registering, you must review and agree to the Synapse governance policies and procedures (Terms and Conditions of Use, Privacy Policy, Awareness and Ethics Pledge). See the Synapse Commons Governance Overview for details.

2

Agree to abide by the Synapse guiding principles for responsible research use and data handling.

3

Agree to include the following acknowledgement statement in publications: "The results published here are in whole or in part based on data obtained from the AMP-AD Knowledge Portal accessed at doi:10.7303/syn2580853."

4

Read and accept any 'Data Specific Conditions for Use' when prompted.

5

Complete Data Use Certificate (DUC) steps if neccessary.
