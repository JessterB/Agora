Samples from the ROSMAP study were used for this analysis. RNA-seq levels from the DLPFC were regressed using an ordinal logistic regression model against three measures of AD pathology or cognitive phenotypes. BRAAK score, which measures neurofibrillary tangles; CERAD, which measures neuritic plaques, and COGDX, which measures the final clinical diagnosis and represents the clinical consensus diagnosis. The resulting plot shows the odds ratio and 95% confidence interval of the estimate. Positive associations are represented by an odds ratio > 1 and negative associations by an odds ratio < 1. Statistical significance of the estimate can be seen by hovering over the dots.
