More than 500 targets have been contributed to Agora. These nominations have been provided by researchers from the National Institute on Aging's Accelerating Medicines Partnership in Alzheimer's Disease (AMP-AD) consortium as well as other research teams. The focus of Agora is to host targets that have been identified using computational analyses of high-dimensional genomic, proteomic and/or metabolomic data derived from human samples. These nominations have been released early in the research discovery cycle in order to facilitate evaluation by the broader research community.
