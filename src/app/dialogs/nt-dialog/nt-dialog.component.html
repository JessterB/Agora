About the Nominated Targets

This initial list of nominated targets was contributed by researchers from the National Institute on Aging's (NIA) Accelerating Medicines Partnership in Alzheimer's Disease (AMP-AD) consortium. The contributing teams used computational analyses of high-dimensional genomic, proteomic and/or metabolomic data derived from human samples to identify genes that might be good targets for AD treatment or prevention. These nominations have been released early in the research discovery cycle in order to collect feedback from the broader research community.
