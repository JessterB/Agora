Where does the data come from?

RNA-Seq data was generated on over 2100 post-mortem brains from over 1100 individuals for seven distinct brain regions (DLPFC, TCX, CBE, PHG, IFG, STG, FP) from three studies (ROSMAP, Mayo, and MSBB). Here we show results of a coexpression network analysis that uses an ensemble methodology to identify networks of genes that show similar coexpression across patients. The more frequent an edge (coexpression association) was identified with the selected gene, the darker the edge and node in the graph.
