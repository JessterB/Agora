Filter by Tissue

The seven brain regions from the AMP-AD studies include Temporal Cortex (TCX), Cerebellum (CBE), Dorsolateral Prefrontal Cortex (DLPFC), Parahippocampal Gyrus (PHG), Frontal Pole (FP), Superior Temporal Gyrus (STG), and Inferior Frontal Gyrus (IFG).

Box Plots

These box plots show the distribution of the effect size (log fold change) and significance (-log10 adjusted p-value) of the selected gene in the context of all genes of the selected tissue and model. The selected gene is shown as the larger red dot.

Filter by Model

We tested for differences in RNA expression between Alzheimer's disease cases and controls using a variety of statistical models. These include looking for both differences in the mean expression of genes between cases and controls, as well as testing for sex specific disease interactions, as well as age of death (AOD) specific interactions.

Log Change Forest Plot

A log forest plot indicates the estimate of the log fold change with standard errors across the tissues and studies where the model was fit.
