Welcome!

The wall of targets is designed for users to discover and explore evidence linking gene's to Alzheimer's disease based on molecular signatures of dementia. Data is derived from multiple human and model system cohorts in the AMP-AD, M2OVE-AD, MODEL-AD, and Resilience-AD consortia that include genome sequencing, RNA-sequencing, proteomics, metabolomic, and more.

View nominated target list

A list of genes nominated by AMP-AD groups as targets of interest. Each AMP-AD team has deployed state of the art systems biology methods to integrate across genomic, transcriptomic, and proteomic data from over 2000 participant brains. Each target represents a gene with multiple lines of evidence and is a candidate driver of Alzheimer disease etiology.
